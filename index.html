How to Generate QR Code with HayamiQR

Creating a QR code with HayamiQR is quick and effortless. Just enter your text or link, click generate, and download your custom QR code — all in a matter of seconds. Follow the simple steps below to get started.

01 Enter Your Text or Link

Start by typing the content you want to convert into a QR code. This can be a website URL or any plain text. Make sure it’s accurate — what you enter here is what your QR code will store.

02 Click the "Generate" Button

Once you've entered your content, click the Generate button. HayamiQR will instantly create a scannable QR code based on your input — no waiting, no loading screens.

03 Download Your QR Code

After the QR code appears, click the Download button to save it as an image. You can now use it in documents, on websites, print materials, or wherever you need.

04 Clear the Input

If you want to create another QR code or start over, click the Clear button. This will reset the input field and remove the previous QR code from view.

The Story Behind HayamiQR

HayamiQR was created with one clear goal — to make QR code generation fast, simple, and accessible for everyone. The name "Hayami" is a Japanese word that means "quick view" or "fast glance," which perfectly reflects our core mission: instant QR codes without the clutter.

Whether you're sharing a link, a short message, or event info, HayamiQR lets you do it in seconds — no sign-ups, no distractions, just pure speed. From the clean interface to the smooth download process, everything is designed to save time and make sharing as efficient as possible.

HayamiQR isn’t just a tool — it’s a philosophy: simple, fast, and focused. That’s why we chose the name “Hayami” — because in a world full of delays, we believe in getting things done, quickly.
